The reason this website exists

Although I Technically made a website (if you could even call it that) in the first year of college, compared that abomination of a site,
this is my first proper attempt. During late 2020 / early 2021 (just coming out of the first lockdowns here in England)
I became heavily interested in Linux and more broadly the Free Software movement.
During this time I began watching lots of Linux related content on youtube, including Luke Smith.
Over the next few months, I must have consumed most if not all of his videos, learnt lots about DWM,
and infused my brain with endless obscure trivia. Sometime at the end of this youtube-binge I found Luke's video on setting up nginx,
and thought I'd salvadge something from my college project.
Anyway, I've left this site dormant for too long now, its time I actually add some content.
